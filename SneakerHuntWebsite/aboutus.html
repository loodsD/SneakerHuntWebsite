About Us

Sneakerhunt.com.my[the "Website"] is created by student from UMT(University Malaysia Terengganu) from Malaysia. This website contains variety of sneakers from different brands such as adidas, nike, vans and puma. User can explore the current trending sneakers comprising renowned brands. Sneakerhunt.com.my will navigate user to the official website is user click on the sneakers. Sneakerhunt.com.my provide random sneaker generator to generate sneakers randomly that might be captivating for users to choose for their next purchase.
